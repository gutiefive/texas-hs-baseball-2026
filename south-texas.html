South Texas Region Outlook

South Texas represents the state's most geographically diverse baseball region, spanning from San Antonio to the Rio Grande Valley to the coastal Corpus Christi area. Calallen's defending 4A state championship and Smithson Valley's state finalist status anchor the region. The area features elite prospects including Aiden Verette (Smithson Valley, All-State), Drayton Mitchell (Calallen, state champ), and a deep pipeline of talent throughout the 6A, 5A, and 4A classifications. The Rio Grande Valley remains competitive with Sharyland (#9 state), while Coastal Bend programs like Calallen dominate 4A. Multiple district champions including La Joya Palmview (5x consecutive title) showcase consistent excellence throughout the region.
