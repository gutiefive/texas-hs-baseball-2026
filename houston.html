Houston Region Outlook

The Greater Houston region features the defending 6A-DII state champions in Kingwood and a competitive landscape of strong programs including Tomball, Pearland, Memorial, and Bridgeland. The region is loaded with current talent: Graham Charboneau (Baylor commit, State MVP), Karson Reeder (Texas commit), CJ Sampson (Texas A&M commit), and rising sophomore phenom Lubin Rincon (#1 Texas 2027 prospect, Shadow Creek/Pearland). Expect an exciting 2026 season with multiple competitive playoff matchups.
