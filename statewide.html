2026 Texas High School Baseball Season

The 2026 Texas HS baseball season features unprecedented depth with national-level talent across the state. Grady Emerson (Fort Worth Christian) projects as a first-round draft pick, while elite arms like Savion Sims (Prestonwood) and Cole Koeninger (Keller) provide two-way competition at the highest level. Defending champions Kingwood and Calallen anchor the playoff landscape, with Lake Travis's Webb-McBride duo representing arguably the nation's best high school pitching combination. The state spans from Dallas to the Rio Grande Valley with competitive programs at every classification level. Multiple regions feature state tournament experience and returning talent positioning 2026 as an exciting competitive season across Texas.
