Central Texas Outlook

Austin and Central Texas feature one of the most talent-rich pitching landscapes in the state with Webb and McBride at Lake Travis forming arguably the nation's best high school pitching duo. Blake Cox's elite ERA at Dripping Springs adds another dimension. The region also boasts talented position players with Westlake's Austin Knox leading defensively and Austin Anderson's Connor Comeau (Texas A&M commit, .345 AVG, 6 HR) anchoring a dangerous offense. Vandegrift's loaded junior/sophomore classes promise future dominance. Expect competitive playoff races and excellent baseball throughout the 2026 season.
